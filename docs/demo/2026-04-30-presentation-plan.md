## 1. The one sentence the deck is built around

The deck is built around a sentence Jiri Nagarpalika already wrote
about itself, on its own city profile page:

> *"नगरमा आउने पर्यटकहरूले यस्ता कृषि उपजलाई खोजी खोजी उपहारको रुपमा
> लैजाने गरेका छन् ।"*
>
> *"Tourists who come to the city actively seek out these agricultural
> products and take them as gifts."*
>
> — jirimun.gov.np, परिचय › संक्षिप्त परिचय

Every other slide either earns the right to quote that sentence, or
extends it. **The pitch is: "you said this. We are the durable
infrastructure that makes it true year-round, after the tourist
goes home."**

When in doubt, return to that sentence.

## 2. Framing — three reframes that lift the whole pitch

### Reframe A — From "help us build" to "your slogan, scaled"
Jiri's own self-description is *"क्लिन जिरी, ग्रीन जिरी, अर्गानीक जिरी"*
and the city profile flatly declares Jiri an **"अर्गानिक नगर"
(organic city)**. The deck never invents new branding. The mark we
ask to license is **"अर्गानीक जिरी"** — their phrase, their
declaration, attached to JiriSewa-verified produce. Lower friction
than asking them to bless something new.

### Reframe B — From "post-disaster" to "income-side complement"
Earthquake reconstruction is still the first item on every page's
right-rail, 11 years on. Per-ward grievance-resolved beneficiary
rosters are still being published. **Do not say "post-disaster."
Do not say "recovery is over."** The platform is the income-side
complement to physical rebuild — cash flowing to households whose
homes are still being completed.

### Reframe C — From "Jiri only" to "Jiri first, Nepal next"
Officials prefer "be first" over "be only." Jiri = pilot for what
753 municipalities can adopt. Jiri's name travels with the model
as it scales. Slide 1 establishes this. Slide 12 closes with it.

## 3. Goals (in priority order)

1. **Mayor's verbal authorization** to add JiriSewa as a respondent
   to the existing **Koseli Ghar (कोशेली घर)** call for proposals,
   *or* to host the pickup hub alongside Koseli Ghar. The Koseli
   Ghar call is currently pinned to the homepage notice ticker —
   we are not asking for a new program, we are responding to one
   they have already published.
2. **Mayor's verbal commitment** to license the **"अर्गानीक जिरी"**
   phrase as a quality mark on verified JiriSewa-listed Jiri
   produce. Non-exclusive, revocable, quality-conditional.
3. **Agreement to take the bilingual MoU** to the next Nagar
   Karyapalika meeting (date specified on the day) for signing.
   We leave a signature-ready document on the table.
4. (Stretch) **Follow-up scheduled** with Agriculture Branch — even
   though the named technician श्रेया आर्याल is flagged "former
   employee," the post still functions through the Mayor's office.

The fuel-subsidy ask from earlier drafts is **removed**. MoU
research established that any subsidy contaminates the document
into procurement-grant territory and blocks the Mayor's unilateral
signature. Pursue separately later.

## 4. What officials need to walk away believing

A. **"This is real. Right now. With our names on it."**
   Live demo, Mitra Bahadur Jirel's name on screen if he's seeded
   as a test farmer, real produce, real prices. The municipality
   sees its own ward chairs' phone numbers reflected in the
   notification UI in the demo.

B. **"This is *our* slogan, *our* program, *our* tourists, scaled
   into a year-round system."** Every slide leans on something
   the municipality has already published.

C. **"What we are giving costs us nothing. The risk is asymmetric
   in our favour."** The municipality risks an address and a
   phrase. The platform risks operational expense and reputation.
   If JiriSewa fails, Jiri loses nothing. If JiriSewa succeeds,
   Jiri's name leads the model into 753 other municipalities.

If a slide doesn't move A, B, or C, it gets cut. Section 8 lists
which slide moves which belief.

## 6. Why Slidev (in 30 seconds, then on to substance)

Markdown like Marp, but Vue components inline. Needed for §10
(live Leaflet hub map + live Supabase counters with
pre-cached fallback) and for the demo iframes on §4, §8, §9. Same
`.md` source → PDF leave-behind, hosted SPA, PNGs of every slide.
Marp can't host live components; everything else (Reveal, Pitch,
MDX-deck) is heavier without a meaningful win here.

## 7. Visual language — anchored in Jiri's own identity

- **Type:** Inter (English) + Mukta (Devanagari), matched stroke
  contrast. Both bundled local with Google CDN fallback.
- **Three-colour theme** drawn from "क्लिन जिरी, ग्रीन जिरी, अर्गानीक जिरी":
  - **Clean** = #F8FAFC (background, breathing room)
  - **Green** = #16A34A (accents, "अर्गानीक जिरी" mark, success states)
  - **Organic** = #2563EB (primary, links, JiriSewa wordmark — kept
    blue because we already use it; "organic" here = the *idea* of
    cleanly-sourced, not a literal earth tone that would muddy the
    mark)
- **No clipart. No stock photography.** Only: app screenshots,
  real produce photos from listings, one map.
- **Bilingual layout:** Devanagari on top in heavier weight,
  English subline beneath at 60% opacity. Not side-by-side
  translation rows.
- **Numbers get their own slide.** "16,109 residents" / "342 kg"
  is the whole slide, big, with one line of context.
- **Maps:** every map pinned to **Jiri Bazaar (27.6275, 86.2202)**
  — the city centre coords from the corpus, *not* the hub seed
  coords from earlier.
- **Tourist places by name** on the closing slide, anchoring Jiri
  in officials' visual memory of their own ward (Buddha Park,
  Jata Pokhari, Stone Park, Jireshwori Mahadev).
- **Honour Jirel-led governance** — 6 of 9 ward chairs and the
  Mayor are Jirel ethnicity. The deck's tone honours that without
  making a thing of it.
